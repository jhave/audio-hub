55 BPM drag-tempo core-trap-cloud-hop ballad, 
gothic-Americana meets hauntology dub, 
baritone croon verses, incantatory speak-sing, 
gravel-whisper spoken bridge, quivering falsetto 
on final refrains. 808 sub-pulse slow as breath, 
wet unquantized triplet hats, cathedral reverb tail, 
vinyl crackle carpet, tape warble, shortwave hiss. 
Rhodes piano bowed with cello bow, prepared piano 
with paper clips on hammers, pedal steel tuned 
quartertone flat, harmonium drone rooted A-flat, 
detuned FM bells 7 cents sharp, granular pad from 
fermentation field recording, distant cicadas and 
kettle. Close-mic vocal, lips audible, breath left in, 
consonants wet. Structure: loose verse, ragged chorus, 
verse, ragged chorus, spoken bridge with tape stop, 
final chorus fraying into hiss and harmonium drone. 
No drop, no build, no four-on-floor, no autotune 
sheen, no festival swell, no anthemic lift, 
no cinematic crescendo, no sidechain pump.
